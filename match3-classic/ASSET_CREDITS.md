## Colorblind Accessibility Notes

The gem set uses 4 distinct shapes to ensure colorblind players can differentiate pieces:
1. **Diamond** - Pointed rotated square shape
2. **Hexagon** - Six-sided polygon
3. **Rectangle** - Wide horizontal bar
4. **Square** - Compact square shape

When shapes are repeated, maximally contrasting colors are used:
- Diamond: Blue vs Grey
- Hexagon: Red vs Purple
